What is Acw all about?

Anticockwise (Acw) is a crew of Musicians whom had a vision it all Started as a dream Then the dream became a reality when the boys decided to take things to the booth, at Question Music a emerging recording label situated in Parow.
The acw crew consists of three Members Namely Clizer, Russky, and Lani, they recorded their First hit track called "Out gone Hustling" featuring one of the hotest upcoming Star Benzin the bushman. The song was played several times at Bushradio.
And it was doing well on the Digital platform such as, number one music, Reverbnation, AudioMack, SoundCloud sites the track went on the #1 spot several times but the video will be available soon.
The crew have been involved in quit a lot of live performance lately in the Cape, firstly it was SAADA festival in Nyanga junction, Auther Mafokate was also involved in the event
The boys visited couple of BushRadio Cyphers, interviews, and Club Performance, Chez ntemba VIP club, in Cape town, and many more.
